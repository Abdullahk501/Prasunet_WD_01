ECL HALL
BOSTON


ECL Hall features a blend of modern and traditional
architectural elements, creating a visually appealing and
harmonious design. The exterior often incorporates clean lines,
large windows, and elegant detailing, making it a standout

02

LOUIS HOUSE,
PORTLAND


The Louis House in Portland is a beautiful and impressive
architectural marvel. Its design blends modern aesthetics with
classic elements, creating a harmonious and visually stunning
structure. The house's clean lines, expansive windows, and
elegant use of materials allow it to stand out while seamlessly
integrating with its surroundings.

03

YC TOWER,
SAN DIEGO


The YC Tower in San Diego is an exquisite example of contemporary
architecture. Its sleek, towering structure commands attention,
blending seamlessly into the urbanskyline. The facade, made of
glass and steel, reflects the city's vibrant atmosphere, while
offering panoramic views of San Diego.

LAYTON & TAYLOR
ARCHITECTS

Layton & Taylor Architects is a renowned architectural firm known for its innovative designs and
commitment to excellence.
Our vision is to create inspiring and enduring spaces that blend creativity with functionality.

Services:
Architectural Design: Custom design solutions for various projects.
Urban Planning: Sustainable and vibrant urban environments.
Interior Design: Maximizing space and aesthetic appeal.
Project Management: Timely and budget-conscious delivery.

Notable Projects:
YC Tower, San Diego: A striking contemporary tower with luxurious interiors.
Louis House, Portland: A modern residential masterpiece combining elegance and sustainability.
ECL Hall, Boston: An iconic public building for community and cultural events.

Commitment:
We prioritize sustainability by using eco-friendly materials and green technologies. Our client
-centered approach ensures tailored solutions that meet and exceed expectations, creating spaces that reflect
our clients' visions and goals.

Layton & Taylor Architects transforms ideas into reality, delivering architectural works that inspire and endure.
